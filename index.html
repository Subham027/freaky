THE FREAKY NIGGERS

In the dead of night, when even the bravest refuse to walk the streets, The Freaky Niggers emerge from the darkness. Ruthless, unpredictable, and feared by all, this gang is more than just a name whispered in fear—they are a force of chaos. Smugglers, thieves, and killers, they operate beyond the limits of morality, carving their legacy in blood and terror.
Authorities have tried to bring them down, but The Freaky Niggers don’t just disappear—they hunt. From illegal arms deals to unspeakable acts of violence, their reach extends far beyond the streets, infecting every layer of the city’s underworld. If you cross them, there are only two outcomes: run, or pray they don’t find you first..
